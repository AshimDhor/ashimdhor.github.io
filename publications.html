TUNE++: Topology-Guided Uncertainty Estimation for Reliable 3D Medical Image Segmentation
Ashim Dhor, Abhirup Banerjee, Tanmay Basu
Medical Imaging with Deep Learning (MIDL) 2026
Paper Code
We propose TUNE++, a topology-guided framework for uncertainty-aware 3D medical image segmentation. By integrating persistent homology-based topological features with a dual attention mechanism, TUNE++ captures both geometric and topological properties of anatomical structures. Our method estimates voxel-wise uncertainty through a topology-uncertainty-aware paired attention block that jointly models spatial, channel, and topological attention along with an uncertainty module. Extensive experiments on multiple 3D medical imaging benchmarks demonstrate that TUNE++ achieves state-of-the-art segmentation accuracy while providing reliable uncertainty estimates that correlate with actual prediction errors.
HISTO-UNet: Histopathology Image Segmentation Using Topology-Aware UNet with Dual Uncertainty Quantification
Ashim Dhor, Emily Das, Rasel Mondal, Sweta Azad, Vaishali Walke, Shakti Kumar Yadav, Abhirup Banerjee, Tanmay Basu
IEEE International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging (ISBI) 2026
Paper Code
We present HISTO-UNet, a topology-aware UNet architecture for histopathology image segmentation that integrates dual uncertainty quantification. The model combines persistent homology-based topology loss with both epistemic and aleatoric uncertainty estimation to produce reliable segmentation maps and calibrated uncertainty scores for clinical decision support in digital pathology workflows.
Topo-GraT: Learning to Grow with Causal Graph Transformers
Ashim Dhor, Smily Bharadwaj
AAAI 2026 — Student Abstract Track
Paper
We introduce Topo-GraT, a causal graph transformer framework that leverages topological data analysis for learning structural growth patterns. The model discovers and exploits causal relationships in graph-structured data, enabling interpretable and robust predictions for dynamic graph evolution tasks.
An Uncertainty-Aware Deep Learning Model for Gland Segmentation in Prostate Histopathology Images
Ashim Dhor, Emily Das
AAAI 2026 — Empowering Global South AI Community Activity
Paper
We present an uncertainty-aware deep learning framework for automated gland segmentation in prostate histopathology images. The model combines epistemic uncertainty via Monte Carlo Dropout with a novel aleatoric uncertainty approach that injects Gaussian noise into model logits, enabling clinicians to identify regions where the model's predictions may be unreliable.

Financial Fraud Detection Using Quantum Graph Neural Networks
N. Innan, A. Sawaika, A. Dhor, S. Dutta, S. Thota, H. Gokal, N. Patel, M. A. Khan, I. Theodonis, M. Bennai
Quantum Machine Intelligence, 2024
Paper
We propose a Quantum Graph Neural Network (QGNN) for financial fraud detection that leverages Topological Data Analysis to process transaction graphs and a Variational Quantum Circuit to enhance feature learning. The proposed QGNN significantly outperforms classical GraphSAGE baselines, achieving 94.5% accuracy and a Precision-Recall AUC of 0.85, demonstrating quantum advantage on the fraud detection task.
