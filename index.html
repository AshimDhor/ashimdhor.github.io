Ashim Dhor

BS-MS Student in Data Science & Engineering, IISER Bhopal

I am Ashim, a final-year BS-MS student in the Department of Data Science and Engineering at IISER Bhopal. I am currently working on my Master's thesis in the Biomedical Data Science Lab under the supervision of Dr. Tanmay Basu, in collaboration with clinicians at JNCH Bhopal and AIIMS Bhopal.

My research focuses on medical image analysis, uncertainty quantification, and topology-aware deep learning for reliable clinical AI systems. I am particularly interested in building interpretable and trustworthy models for computational pathology and 3D biomedical segmentation.

I am also a Bioinformatics Scientist Intern at Elucidata, where I work on large-scale multi-omics data pipelines.
